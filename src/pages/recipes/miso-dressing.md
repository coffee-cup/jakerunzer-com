Dressing for any occasion! My favourite is on a kale salad or couscous bowl.

## Ingredients

- Miso paste
- Honey
- Olive oil
- Soy sauce
- Rice vinegar
- Garlic
- Salt and pepper

## Method

Mix it all together until it tastes good.
