Way better than store bought. Perfect for tacos or quesadillas.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 3/4 cup water
- 3tbsp oil
- salt and sugar to taste

## Method

1. Mix all ingredients together until doughy
2. Leave for 10 minutes
3. Roll out flat and into tortilla shape
4. Fry on high heat without oil
