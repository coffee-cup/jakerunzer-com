## Ingredients

- 500g fresh peeled shrimp
- 1 ripe mango, diced
- 1 ripe avocado, diced
- 250ml cucumber, diced
- 1/2 medium red onion, finely diced
- 250ml cilantro, chopped
- 1 small jalapeno, seeded and finely chopped
- 75ml lime juice (about 2 limes)
- 1tsp lime zest
- 2tsp tabasco sauce
- 1tbsp olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Method

1. Place the shrimp in a colander and press down a little to remove any excess moisture
2. Transfer shrimp to a large bowl
3. Add all other ingredients and mix together gently
4. Serve garnished with cilantro leaves and some lime wedges
