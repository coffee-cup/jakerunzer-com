# Grilled Cheese

yum yum.

## Ingredients

- Bread
- Butter
- Cheese

## Method

1. Butter one side of each piece of bread
2. Cut the desired amount of cheese
3. Place bread on the (hot) frying pan, butter side out
4. Fry until the desired meltiness. Be careful not to burn.
5. Flip carefully
6. Fry until done
7. Cut at a diagonal
8. Enjoy
