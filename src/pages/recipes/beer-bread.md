Make a loaf or biscuits

## Ingredients

- 12 oz can of beer
- 1/3 cup of white sugar
- 3 cups of flour
- 3 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
- 1/4 cup melted butter (brush on top)
- Spices (rosemary works well)

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 375
2. Fix all ingredients together until doughy
3. Add some spices
3. Portion dough onto cooking sheet/stone
4. Brush with butter
5. Bake at 375 for 50 minutes
